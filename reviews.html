Reviewing: Dune: Part Two
★★★★★
Posted 2 hours ago

An absolute masterpiece of modern cinema. The visual scale and sound design are unparalleled. Villeneuve has managed to adapt the unadaptable once again. The pacing is fantastic, giving characters room to breathe while the action set pieces are awe-inspiring. A must-watch in IMAX if you can.
