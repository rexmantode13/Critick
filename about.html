About Critick

We believe movies are more than entertainment — they are a shared cultural experience. Critick was built to give film lovers a beautiful, modern platform to discover, track, and discuss cinema from around the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Critick free to use?

Yes! Creating an account, rating movies, and writing reviews are completely free. Upgrade to Pro for the full experience.

Where do you get your movie data?

We use TMDB poster data for high-quality artwork. The platform is designed to integrate fully with TMDB/OMDB APIs.

How do I delete a review?

Go to your Profile page, open the Activity timeline, and select the Delete option next to any of your reviews.
