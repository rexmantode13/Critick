Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel at any time from your account settings. Your Pro benefits remain active until the end of the current billing period.
What happens after the 7-day free trial? +
After the trial ends, you'll be automatically charged based on your selected billing cycle. We'll send you a reminder 24 hours before the trial expires.
How does the Yearly plan work? +
With the yearly plan, you're billed once annually at a discounted rate — saving you 30% compared to paying monthly. Your subscription auto-renews each year.
How do I become a Verified Critic? +
Subscribe to the Critic plan and our editorial team will review your profile. Once approved (usually within 48 hours), you'll receive your verified badge and be featured in the Top Critics section.
Is there a student or creator discount? +
Yes! We offer a 50% student discount with a valid .edu email, and a special creator rate for YouTube/Instagram film reviewers. Contact us to apply.
